Understanding TMJ Therapy

Specialized massage therapy for temporomandibular joint dysfunction (TMJD) relief and management.

What is TMJD?

Temporomandibular Joint Dysfunction (TMJD) affects the jaw joint and surrounding muscles that control jaw movement. Common symptoms include:

  • Jaw pain or tenderness
  • Difficulty opening or closing mouth
  • Clicking or popping sounds
  • Facial pain and headaches

How Does TMJ Massage Help?

Our specialized TMJ massage therapy targets the muscles and tissues around the jaw joint through:

  • External jaw and facial massage
  • Intraoral massage techniques (with gloves)
  • Trigger point therapy
  • Gentle stretching exercises

What Results to Expect

Immediate Relief

  • Reduced jaw tension
  • Easier jaw movement
  • Decreased pain

Short-Term Benefits

  • Better jaw mobility
  • Reduced headaches
  • Improved eating comfort

Long-Term Results

  • Sustained pain relief
  • Prevention of flare-ups
  • Better stress management
