What We Offer Our Team

Comprehensive benefits and support for your professional and personal success

Focus on What You Do Best

No laundry duties or extensive cleaning. Simply maintain your treatment space between clients—we handle the rest. Your time and expertise are valued, allowing you to focus entirely on delivering exceptional therapeutic care.

Dedicated Wellness Space

Access to a private wellness room for your own self-care between sessions. Whether you need to stretch, meditate, rest, or simply recharge, this space is exclusively for you—because your well-being matters too.

Performance-Based Earnings

As an independent contractor, compensation is structured through a commission split, discussed during the interview. This model rewards the quality of care you provide and offers flexibility in your earning potential.

Professional Growth Support

Optional business coaching and mentorship opportunities for therapists interested in expanding their skills and building their professional practice. We support your long-term career goals and personal development.

Schedule Flexibility That Works

Choose between part-time or full-time contract positions with collaborative scheduling. While some core hours are needed, we work with you to create a sustainable schedule that supports your work-life balance and personal commitments.

Prime Leduc Location

Practice within a welcoming, high-traffic chiropractic office in Leduc. Benefit from an established patient base in a collaborative multidisciplinary healthcare environment. Shared treatment room with coordinated scheduling ensures your sessions remain uninterrupted.

What We're Looking For

We seek exceptional massage therapists who share our commitment to healing and professional excellence

Essential Requirements

  • Licensed RMT in Leduc Alberta with current registration
  • 2+ years of professional massage therapy experience
  • Professional liability insurance and clean professional record
  • Strong communication skills and client-focused approach
  • Reliable, punctual, and professional presentation

Preferred Qualifications

  • TMJ therapy experience or willingness to train
  • Deep tissue and therapeutic massage specialization
  • Additional certifications in myofascial release, trigger point therapy
  • Experience in clinical or therapeutic settings
  • Bilingual abilities (English/French, English/Mandarin)

Current Opportunities

Registered Massage Therapist

Contract Position


Immediate Start

We're seeking an exceptional Registered Massage Therapist who values their craft and wants to practice in an environment that truly respects their expertise. Located in Leduc within a welcoming, high-traffic chiropractic office, this position offers the support and flexibility that allows you to focus on what you do best—delivering exceptional therapeutic care.

What We Offer

Professional environment: No laundry, no non-standard cleaning—focus solely on your clients
Wellness room access: Dedicated space for your own self-care between clients
Above-average compensation: Your expertise is valued and compensated accordingly
Business coaching: Optional support for professional growth and development
Flexible scheduling: Core hours with collaborative scheduling to ensure work-life balance
Established client base: High-traffic location with immediate demand for services

Note: Treatment room is shared with one other RMT with coordinated scheduling to ensure uninterrupted sessions.

Ideal Candidate Profile

  • Registered Massage Therapist in good standing (2,200 hours or equivalent certification)
  • Specialization in therapeutic modalities including deep tissue work and/or cupping therapy
  • Growth-oriented mindset with commitment to continuous professional development
  • Client-centered approach focusing on therapeutic outcomes and patient care
  • Strong collaborative skills with ability to integrate into a multidisciplinary healthcare setting

Please Note

This opportunity may not be the right fit if you:

  • • Prefer maximizing appointment volume over in-depth therapeutic care.
  • • Are uncomfortable providing attentive, individualized treatment for clients with pain.
  • • Have difficulty collaborating respectfully within a shared clinic environment.

We prioritize a respectful, grounded work environment where professionalism and patient-centered care are paramount.
