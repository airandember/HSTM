Your Personal Wellness Sanctuary

The Haven represents a revolutionary approach to wellness - a secluded space designed for profound relaxation and rejuvenation. Whether you're seeking a peaceful escape from daily stress, or a luxurious add-on to your massage therapy, The Haven offers a unique wellness experience tailored to your needs.

Perfect for clients who want to experience wellness on their own terms - no hands-on therapy required, just pure, personalized tranquility.

The Haven Experiences

Zero Gravity Massage Chair

Experience weightless relaxation in our state-of-the-art zero gravity massage chair. Let advanced technology melt away tension while you float in perfect comfort.

Meditation & Breathwork Station

Center yourself in our dedicated mindfulness space, complete with guided meditation resources and breathwork tools for deep inner peace.

Infrared Sauna 1

Detoxify and rejuvenate with gentle infrared heat therapy. Perfect for muscle recovery, stress relief, and overall wellness enhancement.

Wellness Amenities

Enhance your experience with our curated selection of wellness tools including eye massagers, affirmation cards, and other mindfulness resources.

Personalized Experience

Your Haven experience is entirely customizable. Use it as a standalone wellness retreat or combine with massage therapy for the ultimate relaxation journey.

Evolving Sanctuary

The Haven is always evolving. Your feedback and requests will help us create a space that is truly unique and tailored to your needs.

Just FYI's

1 Sauna Attire Policy at The Haven

For comfort, hygiene, and effectiveness, all guests must wear lightweight, breathable clothing (cotton or natural fibers).
Towels may be used to sit on, but nudity is not permitted in our sauna. Thank you for respecting our shared wellness space.

2 Cancellation Policy

Cancellations made less than 24 hours before your appointment or no-shows may be subject to a fee for the full cost of the missed appointment. A credit card is required to be securely kept on file at the time of booking and may be charged in the event of a late cancellation or no-show. Payment for missed appointments must be settled before booking your next appointment. Please note, cancellation fees cannot be billed to insurance.
