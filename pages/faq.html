Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to common questions about our massage therapy services and policies.

What is TMJ Therapy?

A specialized massage treatment that helps relieve jaw pain and tension headaches.

How long are sessions?

Sessions are available in 30, 60, or 90-minute durations based on your needs.

Do you take insurance?

We provide detailed receipts for insurance reimbursement where applicable.

What should I wear?

Comfortable clothing. We provide proper draping during all treatments.

First Visit Questions?

Arrive 15 minutes early to complete intake forms and discuss your needs.

Cancellation Policy?

24-hour notice required to avoid a cancellation fee.

Payment Methods?

We accept cash, credit cards, HSA/FSA cards, and digital payments.

Gift Certificates?

Available for purchase online or in-person for any service.
