Our Story

Discover how Hibiscus Springs grew from a vision of holistic healing to become Leduc's trusted name in specialized massage therapy.

Our Beginning

Founded in 2020, Hibiscus Springs emerged from a passion for combining traditional massage techniques with modern therapeutic approaches. Our founder, Leslie Fuson, recognized the growing need for specialized TMJ treatment in Leduc's wellness community.

What started as a small practice has blossomed into a comprehensive wellness center, serving hundreds of clients with personalized care and expertise. Our name, inspired by the healing properties of the hibiscus flower, represents our commitment to natural healing and renewal.
