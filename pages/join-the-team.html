What We're Looking For

We seek exceptional massage therapists who share our commitment to healing and professional excellence

Essential Requirements

  • Licensed RMT in Leduc Alberta with current registration
  • 2+ years of professional massage therapy experience
  • Professional liability insurance and clean professional record
  • Strong communication skills and client-focused approach
  • Reliable, punctual, and professional presentation

Preferred Qualifications

  • TMJ therapy experience or willingness to train
  • Deep tissue and therapeutic massage specialization
  • Additional certifications in myofascial release, trigger point therapy
  • Experience in clinical or therapeutic settings
  • Bilingual abilities (English/French, English/Mandarin)
