Our Beginning

Hibiscus Springs was born from a season of transformation and a desire to create something beautiful, healing, and empowering.

My journey into massage therapy began over 20 years ago when I was working as a health care aide. I had always been fascinated by the human body; how it functions, how it holds emotion, and how it responds to care. Even before I experienced massage for myself, I knew I wanted to be part of a profession that helped people reconnect with their bodies in a meaningful, restorative way.

The name Hibiscus Springs came to me with clear intention. I wanted something powerful, beautiful, and deeply aligned with the feeling I hoped to create. The hibiscus flower - bold, tropical, and rich with symbolic meaning - represents strength, grace, and divinity. Springs evokes a place of renewal, calm, and flow.
